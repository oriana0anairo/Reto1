Surrogacy is growing and so should your surrogacy journey options. Introducing The Biggest Ask – the first full-service surrogacy community.

When intended parents and surrogates have more control over their journeys, intended parents can save thousands of dollars and surrogates can earn more compensation, and both parties can feel more confident that they found the right surrogacy partner.

We offer a completely free on-line course about how a typical surrogacy journey works in the USA catered to both intended parents and surrogates.
